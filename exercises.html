Don't set too long, do some exercises!

Exercise 1

First excercise twisting and extending your wrists.
Make sure that your head is in a natural position.
Repeat this exercise 5 times.

Exercise 2

The second excercise is back extentions.
Put your fist behind and extend your back.
Repeat this exercise 5 times.

Exercise 3

The third excercise is chin tucks.
Standup and pack your neck.
Repeat this exercise 5 times.

Exercise 4

The fourth excercise is side bends.
Put your hands on your sides and do a side bending.
Repeat this exercise 5 times on each side.
